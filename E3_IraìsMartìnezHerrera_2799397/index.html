Crime Series



What are crime series?

A crime drama is a type of drama show which has as its primary focus or heroes people whose main occupation is to investigate, punish or commit crimes—especially homicide, assault, robbery or racketeering. Notable crime dramas in TV history include Dragnet, Hill Street Blues, Homicide: Life on the Street, NYPD Blue, Law and Order, The Sopranos and CSI.
